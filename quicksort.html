Quick sort

How it works:

Quick sort is a divide-and-conquer algorithm.It works by selecting a 'pivot' element from the array and partitioning the other elements into two sub-arrays, according to wheter they are less than or greater than the pivot.The sub-arrays are then sorted recursively.

The algorithm has a time complexity of O(n log n) and a space complexity of O(n log n).
